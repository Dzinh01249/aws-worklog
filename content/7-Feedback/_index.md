> Prompt: Review the internship experience through the lens of the group project, then suggest improvements that help both the FCJ program and the team’s delivery.

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is friendly and open. FCJ members are willing to help whenever I encounter difficulties, even outside working hours. The workspace is tidy and comfortable, which helps me focus better on the group project. Still, I believe more team bonding sessions would strengthen collaboration and reduce communication friction.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentor provides detailed guidance, explains concepts clearly, and encourages me to ask questions. The admin team supports project documentation and logistics, making it easier for our group to stay on track. I appreciate that the mentor lets us try and solve problems ourselves rather than just giving answers.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The assigned tasks align well with my university knowledge while introducing new domains such as cloud deployment and infrastructure operations. This helped me strengthen fundamentals and gain practical skills directly useful for the group project.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
I learned new skills in project management tools, teamwork, and professional communication. The mentor’s real-world examples also helped me shape a clearer roadmap for my career and for contributing better to the project.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The company culture is positive: people respect one another, work seriously, but also keep the atmosphere friendly. When urgent tasks arise, everyone cooperates and supports each other, which makes me feel like a real team member despite being an intern.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The company offers an internship allowance and flexible working hours when needed. The chance to join internal training sessions is also a strong benefit.
